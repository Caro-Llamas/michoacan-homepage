Visit Michoacan

Located in western Mexico, is a tapestry of indigenous heritage, vibrant festivals, and culinary delights.

Top three places to visit at Michoacan

Morelia

The capital of Michoacan offers stunning colonial architecture, vibrant cultural heritage, delicious cuisine, and rich history.

Páztcuaro

This charming colonial town is famous for its picturesque streets, traditional crafts, and cultural festivals.

The Monarch Butterfly Biosphere Reserve

This sanctuary has millions of monarch butterflies that migrate from Canada and the United States each year.

Foods that you should definitely try

Carnitas

Carnitas are tender, slow-cooked pork, seasoned to perfection, offering a flavorful, crispy yet juicy delight.

Enchiladas

This dish features handmade tortillas dipped in rich chili sauce, stuffed with cheese or chicken, and topped with potatoes.

Chongos Zamoranos

Chongos zamoranos are a sweet, creamy dessert made from curdled milk, cinnamon, and sugar, offering a delicate texture.
